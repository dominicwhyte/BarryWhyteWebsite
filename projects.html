LottoDeal


Presentation: Slides

LottoDeal is a revolutionary new platform that combines the psychological appeal of lotteries with an e-commerce marketplace. Each seller is matched with hundreds of interested micro-bidders who can buy virtual “lottery tickets” for any item. When the item reaches its goal, it is lotteried off, where each bidder has a chance of winning the item proportional to the amount that they bid. This helps buyers bid on items they desire, but don’t necessarily need to win, at a fraction of the cost, all the while allowing sellers to utilize economies of scale to sell their items as fast as possible.

I was a part of the team of 5 that built LottoDeal for a course at Princeton (COS333). We built the app using the MEAN stack, with my main focus being on the backend, implementing an item suggestions algorithm, handling the database and managing the lotteries/payments side of the app. I also got my feet wet with AngularJS for some Front End Dev.

Relayr Office IoT

App: iOS

Welcome to relayr’s Smart Office prototype app, an IoT solution for connecting your surroundings to the internet. See how you can monitor a multitude of systems in one place, from which windows are open in a given building to how many people are inside and the ambient temperature.
